what is web 3
The web is the infrastructure of the internet. It’s a communication, collaboration, and commerce platform connecting people worldwide. Web applications are how we interact with the web. Web 3 is the latest web iteration, and it’s powered by blockchain technology. With blockchain, each transaction is recorded on a public ledger that everyone can see. This transparency makes it very difficult to cheat or defraud someone on the network
